# Business Problem

Insurance companies receive a large number of customer requests every day related to policy inquiries, benefits information, claim processing, claim status tracking, renewals, and escalation requests.

These requests are often handled manually by customer support agents, leading to:
- Long customer wait times
- Increased operational cost
- Higher workload on agents
- Inconsistent responses
- Slower claim and policy service resolution

## Need
There is a need for a secure AI-powered conversational voice agent that can automate common insurance service journeys, authenticate customers, detect user intent, execute backend workflows, and escalate complex issues to live agents with conversation context.
