# Intent Routing Logic

- ** New User Onboarding
 - I want to buy a new policy 
 - I am a new customer 
 - Get me insurance
   
- ** Policy Inquiry    
 - Check my policy
 - Show policy details
 - Policy status

- ** Benefits Information    
 - What benefits do I have?
 - Show my coverage
 - Roadside assistance
   
- ** Claim Status    
 - Check my claim
 - Track claim
 - Claim status    

- ** New Claim Initiation    
 - File a claim
 - Register a claim
 - Report an accident

 - ** Policy Renewal
 - Renew Policy
 - Extend insurance
 - Policy renewal
   
- ** Agent Escalation    
  - Talk to agent 
  - Customer support 
  - Human assistance 

## Routing Design
After authentication, customer utterances are mapped to the correct business flow through intent detection. If the system cannot confidently identify an intent, fallback handling is triggered.
