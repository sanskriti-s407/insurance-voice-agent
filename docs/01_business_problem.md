# Business Problem

Insurance companies handle a high volume of customer requests every day, including policy inquiries, benefits information, claim processing, claim status tracking, policy renewals, customer information updates, new policy purchases, and requests for live agent support.

These requests are traditionally managed by customer support representatives, resulting in several operational challenges:

- Long customer wait times
- Increased operational costs
- High workload on support agents
- Inconsistent customer experiences
- Slower claim and policy resolution
- Manual processing of customer update requests
- Limited availability of self-service options

---

## Need

There is a need for a secure, scalable, and AI-powered conversational voice agent that can automate common insurance service journeys while maintaining a seamless customer experience.

The solution should be capable of:

- Securely authenticating customers
- Understanding customer intent through natural language
- Supporting multiple insurance products (Motor, Health, and Life)
- Managing multiple customer policies
- Automating policy, claims, onboarding, and renewal workflows
- Handling customer information update requests through an approval process
- Proactively notifying customers about upcoming policy renewals
- Seamlessly escalating complex requests to live support agents with complete conversation context
