# User Stories

## US-AUTH-001: Customer Authentication
As an existing customer, I want to securely authenticate myself before accessing my insurance information, so that my personal and policy data remains protected.

## US-NEW-001: Customer Onboarding
As a new customer, I want to explore available insurance products and purchase a suitable policy, so that I can become an insured customer and receive a policy.

## US-POL-001: Multi-Policy Selection
As a customer with multiple insurance policies, I want to select the policy I wish to manage, so that the agent performs actions on the correct policy.

## US-POL-002: Policy Inquiry
As an insurance customer, I want to retrieve my policy information, so that I can understand my policy status, coverage details, and expiry date.

## US-BEN-001: Benefits Information
As an insurance customer, I want to view the benefits and coverage included in my policy, so that I can understand the protection and services available to me.

## US-CLS-001: Claim Status
As an insurance customer, I want to check the status of my insurance claim, so that I can track its progress.

## US-CLM-001: New Claim Initiation
As an insurance customer, I want to register a new insurance claim, so that I can request compensation for an insured incident.

## US-REN-001: Policy Renewal
As an insurance customer, I want to renew my insurance policy, so that my insurance coverage continues without interruption.

## US-REN-002: Proactive Renewal Reminder
As an insurance customer, I want to be notified when my policy is nearing its renewal date, so that I can renew it before it expires.

## US-UPD-001: Customer Information Update
As an insurance customer, I want to submit a request to update my personal information, so that my account details remain accurate and up to date.

## US-UPD-002: Update Request Status
As an insurance customer, I want to track the status of my update request, so that I know whether it has been approved or is still under review.

## US-ESC-001: Human Escalation
As an insurance customer, I want to speak with a live support representative, so that I can receive assistance for requests that cannot be resolved automatically.

## US-CTX-001: Session Continuity
As an authenticated customer, I want the agent to remember my selected policy and conversation context throughout the session, so that I do not have to repeat the same information multiple times.
