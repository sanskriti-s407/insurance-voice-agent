# User Stories

## US-NEW-001: New User Onboarding
As a new customer without an existing policy, I want to explore available insurance products and purchase a suitable policy, so that I can get insured and receive a valid policy ID.

## US-POL-001: Policy Inquiry
As an insurance customer, I want to retrieve my policy information, so that I can understand my policy status, coverage details, and policy expiry date.

## US-BEN-001: Benefits Information
As an insurance customer, I want to view the benefits and coverage included in my policy, so that I can understand the services and protection available to me.

## US-CLS-001: Claim Status
As an insurance customer, I want to check the status of my claim, so that I can know the progress of my claim request.

## US-CLM-001: New Claim Initiation
As an insurance customer, I want to register a new claim, so that I can request compensation for an insured incident.

## US-REN-001: Policy Renewal
As an insurance customer, I want to renew my insurance policy, so that I can continue receiving insurance coverage without interruption.

## US-ESC-001: Agent Escalation
As an insurance customer, I want to speak with a live support agent, so that I can get assistance for issues that cannot be resolved by the AI agent.
